Welcome to A Small World!

Most poeple do not realize how complex and detailed a dolhouse can become.

For example, take a look at Queen Mary's Dolls' House. Queen Mary's Dolls' House is the largest, most beautiful and most famous dolls' house in the world.

In order to build such an elaberate masterpiece, the creator must have access to everything from building materials like flooring and wallpapers, to furniture of all kinds, and the tiny accessories that bring their 1/12 scale models to life!

Finding all of these items can be quite a challange! Like any industry, materials are made by various manufacturers and artists. The best solution is to find a miniature store in your area. The DMMA, or Dollhouse and Miniature Merchatns Association has a map with all US based brick and morter stores.

If you are lucky enough to live in St. Louis, Missouri, and anywhere nearby, McGhee's Miniatures is an incredible full line Dollhouse and Miniatures Store and Studio!
